What We Do

We focus on seed and early stage investment to create maximum impact for our companies, investors, and partners.

Our Process

Through our global network of teams, co-investors and partners, we receive a large volume of high quality proposals. We understand and respect that time is scarce for everyone and strive to give quick feedback and answers.
Our emphasis is on maximum support at key inflection points. We do not pretend to have the answers for every question or problem, but our diverse cross-border network allows us to quickly connect with someone who does.
Capital efficient businesses led by remarkable teams have more flexibility about exit opportunities. Rather than pressure founders for either an early exit or a large exit, we support founders in whichever decision they want to pursue.
